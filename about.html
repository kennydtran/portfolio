About

Background

I'm a Computer Science student at California State University, Fullerton, set to graduate in May 2025. My journey in tech has been driven by a passion for solving algorithms and creating meaningful projects.

Growing up, I had the privilege of accessing new technology at a young age. My father was studying in the field of computers and took me along with him through the journey. As I received my first computer at four years old, I was able to learn the basics of operating systems and computer hardware at a very young age. Although my computer was significantly slower than the computers of my peers, this forced me to find ways to make the most out of the hardware. My struggles of owning an older computer was the initial spark that ignited my interest in computers.
